District Policy:  What policies do you think are currently working within the district and which would you change?

On Thursday, September 29, 2022, I participated in the Conversations with Candidates series at Hotel Carmichael and presented by the Carmel Education Foundation, the Carmel Public Library Foundation, and the OneZone Chamber of Commerce. Jack Russell was our gracious moderator.



Most of our current policies are working. Some that come to mind include

  • Administration (1000),
  • School programs (5000),
  • budgeting, and fiscal planning policies (6000).

Our Superintendent and his leadership are responsible for implementing our budget and fiscal planning policies, which allow our school board to focus on monitoring and approval.

We have an opportunity to change the policies regarding our district’s relationship with parents and advocacy groups. These policies are grouped in policy series 9000.

If elected, I will work with our policy committee to update our “Relations” policies so that our school can take a “conciliatory and united approach” with parents and new advocacy groups.

We can transform these groups into friends and supporters of Carmel Clay schools and avoid disrupting our learning environment and good governance of our school board.

If we fail, more parents will leave our schools, and the consequences will be real… and as articulated by our Director of Community Relations at the recent Valor Academy public hearing:

  • Reduction in funding
  • Fewer teachers
  • Larger class sizes
  • Fewer opportunities
  • Reduction in extracurricular activities.

I am the only candidate with a platform that includes “one team” – it is a team that includes all parents and all stakeholders of our community. My proposal will unite us around our district’s “Together we achieve” vision.

In a world of choices, it is Together, as “One Team,” we will ensure that Carmel Clay Schools remain THE place where:

  • teachers are respected
  • families are heard
  • students are inspired and motivated to achieve academic excellence.
