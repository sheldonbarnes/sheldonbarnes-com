Platform

Mental Health Support Group

I have a vision for a district wide PTO mental health support group that will increase equity and inclusion.

My vision will build a better experience for families in our special education program.

The group would host monthly meetings that bring together advocates, experienced parents, special education teachers and staff, mental health providers like St. Vincent and ASPIRE, Parent Project representatives, and others in our community.

11% of our student population have a disability. Our equity and inclusion efforts should include measurable outcomes for acceptance in college and finding a good job.

Watch candidate forum video on mental health
Support our Teachers

I will partner with our legislators to ensure future salary increases for our teachers are competitive with other career choices.

Inspire high school students to pursue the higher calling in the teaching profession.

Ensure we continue to have a solid plan to recruit and retain the best teachers.

Watch candidate forum video on supporting our teachers

One Team

Maintain "One Team" between Superintendent and School Board built on transparency and accountability.

Political action committees from the left and right are trying to divide our community.

The work of our school board is nonpartisan and I will work to keep it that way.

Watch candidate forum video on uniting our community

Parents will be heard at our school board meetings.
