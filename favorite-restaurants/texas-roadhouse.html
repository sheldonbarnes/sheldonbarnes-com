Texas Roadhouse on 14758 Greyhound Plaza, Carmel, IN 46032

My favorite restaurant is Texas Roadhouse on 14758 Greyhound Plaza, Carmel, IN 46032. Phone number 317-795-0511.

My favorite meal is the 23oz Porterhouse T-bone steak topped with sauteed mushrooms and served with green beans and seasoned rice.

My favorite drink is their Jamaican Cowboy!

The Texas Roadhouse rolls are addicting and make it hard to leave room for my favorite dessert, The Big Ol’ Brownie.

The employees at Texas Roadhouse work together as a team, and their managers walk around the restaurant to ensure that diners are satisfied with their meal and experience.
