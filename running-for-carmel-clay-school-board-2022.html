Sheldon Barnes

Sheldon's Platform

Mental Health Support Group

I have a vision for a district wide PTO mental health support group.

The group would host monthly meetings that bring together advocates, experienced parents, special education teachers and staff, mental health providers like St. Vincent and ASPIRE, Parent Project representatives, and others in our community.

Support our Teachers

Partnering with our legislators to ensure future salary increases for our teachers are competitive with other career choices.

Inspire high school students to pursue the higher calling in the teaching profession.

Ensure we continue to have a solid plan to recruit and retain the best teachers.


One Team

Maintain "One Team" between Superintendent and School Board built on transparency and accountability.

Ensure we keep the best interest of the District and academic excellence as the main focus.

Parental Involvement

Parents do more than enhance the learning experience.

Parents are our first teachers and are part of our "One Team".

Parents will be heard at our school board meetings.

Soft Skills

Our students will learn the in-demand skills to interact effectively and harmoniously with others.

Principles and Values

Sound principles and values like "respect for people" are enduring.

Career Pathways

Career Pathways help connect students to career exploration and development

Learn more about how an internship led to my career in Information Technology

Learn more
Academic Excellence

Only the best is good enough!

“Education is the only way out of darkness, into the glorious light.” -Great Debators

Our schools continue to be some of the best perform in our Nation. We should keep them that way.

Civility and Decorum

Whether in-person or on social media, I support our district's focus on "returning our school environments to places where civil discourse is the norm and school personnel, parents, and community members have the opportunity to be heard and respected."
